Banh Xeo

Banh Xeo is a Vietnamese sizzling savory crepe or pancake. The name "banh xeo" directly translates to "sizzling cake" for the loud sizzling sound the batter makes when it hits the hot skillet.

Ingredients

    Crepe Batter:
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ½ cup water
    Fillings:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • ¾ pound fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce, or more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 pound mung bean sprouts
  • 4 lettuce leaves, or as needed

Recipe

  • 1. Make the crepe batter. Mix batter ingredients in a bowl and mix.
  • 2. Make the filling: start frying shallots, garlic and shrimp. After 3-4 minutes season with fish sauce and sauce.
  • 3. Add oil to a pan and make crepes. Layer fillings as if cooks. Fold and flip
  • 4. Serve Banh Xeo alongside additional fillings and toppings

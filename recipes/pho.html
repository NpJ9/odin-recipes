Pho

What Is Pho and What Is It Made Of? Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, and thinly sliced meat (usually beef). It may also be served with bean sprouts, fresh herbs, limes, chiles, and other garnishes.

Ingredients

  • Steak / Chicken / Pork
  • Thin rice noodles
  • Fresh onions and ginger
  • Beef stock
  • Star anise, whole cloves, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods, and coriander seeds.
  • Fish sauce
  • Toppings:Herbs, bean sprouts..

Recipe

  • 1. Prep the protein. Slice the beef, chicken or pork as thinly as possible.
  • 2. Make the broth. Char the onions, ginger and toast the spices in a stockpot.
  • 3. Add onion, giger and broth and cover for 30 minutes. Strain out chunks.
  • 4. Prep the noodles. Cook al dente, drain and toss briefly with cold water.
  • 5. Assemble
  • 6. Serve
